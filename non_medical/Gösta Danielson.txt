Gösta Danielson

Gösta Danielson (17 November 1889 – 14 February 1960) was a Swedish athlete.  He competed in the 1908 Summer Olympics in London. In the 800 metres, Danielson did not finish his initial semifinal heat and did not advance to the final.Danielson represented Djurgårdens IF.
