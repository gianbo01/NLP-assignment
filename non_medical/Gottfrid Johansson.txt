Gottfrid Johansson

Gottfrid Johansson (16 March 1891 – 14 April 1962) was a Swedish international football forward and bandy player.As a footballer, Johansson was part of the Djurgården Swedish champions' team of 1915, 1917, and 1920. Johansson made three appearances for Sweden and scored one goals He made his debut in a friendly against Norway in 1914, in which he also scored his only international goal.As a bandy player, Johansson was part of the Djurgården Swedish champions' team in bandy of 1908 and 1912.
