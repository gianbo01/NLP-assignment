Štefan Svitek

Štefan Svitek (January 23, 1960, Podbrezová, Banská Bystrica Region, Czechoslovakia – June 8, 1989, Bratislava, Czechoslovakia) was a Slovak murderer who killed and butchered his pregnant wife and two daughters in 1987. For this crime, Svitek was sentenced to death. He was the last person executed in Czechoslovakia. 
Vladimír Lulek was the last person executed in the Czech part of Czechoslovakia with his death on February 2, 1989.
